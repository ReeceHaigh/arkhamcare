Schizophrenia is common mental illness effecting thousands of people. Arkham Asylum has updated its Schizophrenia Family Checklist in order to help family members diagnose mental illness with their loved ones. Symptoms include:


- Delusions of control and mass influence.
- Hallucinatory voices “talking” with the patient
- Persistent, overvalued thoughts that are untrue, impossible.


If you believe a family member to by experiencing any of these symptoms, contact Arkham today.
